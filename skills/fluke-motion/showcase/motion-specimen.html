The contract

Every demo below runs on one shared requestAnimationFrame loop and one lerp constant (0.16) — cursor, skew, magnetic, marquee all decay at the same rate, so the whole sheet reads as one material. Heavy effects gate behind prefers-reduced-motion. That is the entire award-site orchestration contract, in one page.
